Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your GCES Hostel account and all associated data from our servers.

How to request

  • 1 Send an email to gceshostel@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  • 2 Include your registered name and email address used in the app
  • 3 We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days

What gets deleted: Your name, email address, usage history, and all personally identifiable information stored on our servers.

What may be retained: Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked back to you, as required by law.

Third-party data: Data stored by Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics may be subject to Google's own retention policies.
